2025 Lamborghini Revuelto

Price: $608,358

Basic info:
The Revuelto is Lamborghini's replacement for the highly popular Aventador. As a flagship supercar for the 21st century, it benefits from a healthy dose of electrification. Generating a total of 1,001 hp, the Revuelto combines a good old-fashioned, naturally aspirated V12 with three electric motors, two of which are positioned at the front. It can be recharged in 30 minutes using a Level 2 charger and driven in pure electric mode for about 8 km.
